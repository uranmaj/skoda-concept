GO ON HOLIDAY WITH EVERYTHING YOU NEED

Backseat passengers will appreciate the Sleep Package (only in the estate version), featuring adjustable head restraints so that they can sleep easy on the way. SET OFF FOR THE MOUNTAINS IN COMFORT

The all-new OCTAVIA is perfect for winter sports thanks to 4×4 drive. In the morning, simply turn on the auxiliary heating with your smartphone to heat the car up. The heated windscreen will deal with any snow. Your skis will easily fit through a load-through area behind the rear armrest. The heated steering wheel and four heated seats will warm you up on the way to the slopes.

COMMUTE EASILY IN THE CITY

The OCTAVIA is perfect for everyday travel in city traffic. When you need to park, the Parking Spaces service will advise you where to find a free space. Then you simply let Park Assist find a suitable spot and guide the car into it. If there is a risk of colliding with another vehicle or a cyclist as you get out of the car, the Exit Warning system alerts you to this danger.
